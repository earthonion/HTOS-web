Questions from the Uninitiated

Is this actually a religion?
The Garlic neither confirms nor denies. What we can confirm is that your PS4 and PS5 saves will be processed with the devotion and care that only a consecrated worker console can provide. Also it's April 1st.
What do I need to receive salvation?
A jailbroken PS4 or PS5 with FTP access, and a free account in our congregation. The Clove asks for no tithes — this service is entirely free, sustained by the faithful who donate their consoles as workers.
Which firmware versions are blessed?
For PS4, any jailbreakable firmware up to 13.00 has received the Clove's blessing. For PS5, firmware versions up to 10.01 are within the fold. The worker consoles handle all the sacred crypto, so your save need not match the worker's firmware.
How does the worker system actually work?
The faithful volunteer their jailbroken consoles as worker monks. These tireless machines run in the background, picking up jobs from the queue and processing saves using Sony's actual system libraries. It's a distributed monastery of PlayStations, humming with purpose.
Can I use saves from another believer's account?
That is the very essence of the Resign sacrament. Upload the save, enter your own account ID, and the save is reborn — re-signed so your console recognizes it as your own. The Garlic knows no borders between accounts.
What if no disciples are online?
Your prayer remains in the queue, patiently awaiting a disciple. The Garlic tests the faithful with patience. Usually a worker comes online within minutes, but during the quiet hours, the wait may be longer. Check the dashboard for real-time worker status.
How can I become a disciple (worker)?
If you possess a jailbroken PS4 or PS5 that walks the earth with an internet connection, you can ordain it as a worker. Create an account, visit the Contribute page for your worker key, and follow the sacred installation rites. Your console will serve the congregation while you use it normally — truly selfless.

The Sacred Texts Are Open

Garlic Saves is fully open source — as the First Clove commanded. The worker code, the server, the save tools — all freely given on GitHub. Built by the homebrew community. Blessed by the bulb.
