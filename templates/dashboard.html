Finding your Account ID

  1. Copy any encrypted save from your PS4 to a USB drive
  2. Plug the USB into your PC and navigate to:
    USB:/PS4/SAVEDATA/
  3. You'll see a folder with a 16-character hex name — that's your Account ID

Example: 3e338f700e51db91

Depending on how you derived your account ID, it could be in little-endian or big-endian format.

Click "Swap Endian" if you're having issues with saves showing "account belongs to a different user".
